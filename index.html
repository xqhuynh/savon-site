About Me

Hello world! I'm here to help with all your technology needs.

I learned how powerful technology is during the latter years in college, most likely because it was never taught in K-12. During my senior year, my capstone senior project consisted of myself and two other classmates building a Web app that connected students with tutors. This one project alone showed me what can be achieved through coding.

From that point on, I became obsessed with coding. I'm a firm believe that technology can solve most problems and I strive to contribute to solutions that will make the world a better place for everyone.
